Fastbraces is a fast, safe, easy and affordable way to correct teeth. While traditional braces move the tooth and allow the root to follow, Fastbraces move the root and the tooth at the same time, without resulting in root resorption. Patients can see dramatic results in about a year – and in some instances, just three months! All at half the price of most traditional braces!
